Using the Spectrum Long Pulsed YAG laser — one of the most trusted devices in vascular treatment — Olam Med Spa delivers high-intensity light energy precisely to visible veins. The laser heats the blood vessel wall, causing it to collapse and gradually shrink as the body naturally reabsorbs it. Spider veins, varicose veins, and vascular lesions including cherry angiomas are treated safely and effectively with no surgery and no downtime. 01

PreparationPreparación

The target area is cleansed thoroughly. No anesthesia is required. Your provider positions the YAG laser device precisely over the treatment zone. El área a tratar se limpia a fondo. No se requiere anestesia. Tu proveedor posiciona el dispositivo láser YAG con precisión sobre la zona de tratamiento.

02

Laser TreatmentTratamiento con Láser

High-intensity light energy is delivered in controlled pulses, absorbed by the blood in the vessel. The vessel wall heats, collapses, and closes. Patients typically feel a sensation similar to a rubber band snap against the skin. Se aplica energía luminosa de alta intensidad en pulsos controlados, absorbida por la sangre dentro del vaso. La pared del vaso se calienta, colapsa y se cierra. Los pacientes suelen sentir una sensación similar a un chasquido de goma elástica sobre la piel.

03

Natural ReabsorptionReabsorción Natural

Over the following weeks, the closed vessel shrinks and is naturally eliminated by the body. Multiple sessions spaced 6–12 weeks apart are typically needed for complete clearance. Durante las semanas siguientes, el vaso colapsado se reduce y es eliminado de forma natural por el cuerpo. Generalmente se necesitan múltiples sesiones espaciadas 6–12 semanas para lograr la eliminación completa.

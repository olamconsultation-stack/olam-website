Our sessions for microneedling in Pembroke Pines typically take about 45–60 minutes, including the numbing process, and depending on the size of the area needing treatment. In certain cases you will require multiple sessions, which are generally spaced 2–3 weeks apart, to achieve optimal results.Nuestras sesiones de microagujamiento en Pembroke Pines típicamente toman alrededor de 45–60 minutos, incluyendo el proceso de anestesia y dependiendo del tamaño del área a tratar.
